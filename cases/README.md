# Cases

These case studies show why governed skill systems behave differently from flat prompt or skill collections.

## Included cases

- [Flat Skill Pile vs Governed Flow](./flat-skill-pile-vs-governed-flow.md)  
  Why structure matters before scale.

- [Skill Growth Proposal](./skill-growth-proposal.md)  
  Why not every repeated problem should create a new skill.

- [Handoff and Continuity](./handoff-continuity.md)  
  Why long-horizon collaboration needs explicit continuity design.

## Why this section exists

The goal of these cases is not to provide runnable examples.

The goal is to make the structural logic of governed skill systems easier to inspect:

- why flow governance matters
- why growth should be proposed instead of improvised
- why continuity must be designed for long-horizon collaboration

# Example: Pre-Change Risk And Dependency Scan

## User Request

"Before we touch this system, help me understand what assumptions are shaky and what could be affected."

## Recommended Chain

1. `brain-skill-orchestrator`
2. `controller-work-state-controller`
3. `balance-assumption-audit`
4. `balance-dependency-impact-scan`
5. `leg-verification-runner`

## Why

- the task is governance-first, not implementation-first
- assumptions and blast radius should be clarified before editing
- verification should match the final change risk, not guesswork
